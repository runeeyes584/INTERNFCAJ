### General Evaluation of the Program

**1. Impressions of the program and learning environment**  
The First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) program has successfully cultivated an open, dynamic tech community atmosphere rather than a rigid office environment. This is an ideal setting for students to gain early exposure to AWS cloud infrastructure and master the process of migrating an application from a local environment to a live cloud deployment. The workspace at the AWS Vietnam office is highly professional, providing a comfortable atmosphere that allows interns to maximize their creative potential.

**2. Support from Mentors and the Organizing Committee (Admin Team)**  
The mentor team is incredibly dedicated, constantly ready to provide technical support and project architecture guidance even outside working hours. I highly appreciate their oriented mentoring approach: empowering interns to independently research and experiment with solutions before offering suggestions, which greatly enhances proactive problem-solving skills. Additionally, the Admin Team has been extremely supportive in providing fully equipped AWS accounts, facilitating a smooth practical learning experience.

**3. Alignment with academic major**  
The serverless game development project perfectly aligns with my Software Engineering major. It not only solidified my programming fundamentals but also expanded my knowledge in advanced domains not deeply covered in the university curriculum, such as NoSQL databases (Amazon DynamoDB), real-time communication (API Gateway WebSockets), and cloud cost optimization strategies.

**4. Overall satisfaction with the program**  
I am absolutely satisfied with the program's structured internship roadmap—from the fundamental hands-on Labs in the initial weeks to the autonomous research and full-scale development phase of the Chrono Genesis TCG project in the latter half.

### Additional Feedback

- **What are you most satisfied with during the internship?**  
  The opportunity to directly build and deploy a complete Web application on the AWS cloud using real-world Serverless services. Furthermore, participating in high-quality tech events like the FCAJ Community Day allowed me to expand my professional network with industry experts.

- **What aspects of the program need improvement for future internship cohorts?**  
  The registration and allocation process for in-person office working sessions could be better optimized for fairness. Currently, the randomized selection mechanism sometimes leads to unequal office opportunities among members. A more balanced scheduling approach would ensure everyone has equal chances for in-person collaboration.

- **Would you recommend this internship program to your friends?**  
  Absolutely. This is a highly practical internship program that provides top-tier learning resources and serves as the best current environment for students to comprehensively develop their Cloud computing skills in general, and within the Amazon Web Services ecosystem in particular.

### Proposals & Future Aspirations

- **Experience Proposal:** I hope the organizing team considers refining the approval process for attending training and events at the FCAJ office to provide more interns with practical exposure.
- **Future Aspirations:** I genuinely look forward to the opportunity to continue accompanying and contributing to the program in the future, whether as a mentor supporting upcoming cohorts or by participating in new strategic projects within the FCAJ community.
